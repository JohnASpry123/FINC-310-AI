TABLE 3
Required Step | Required Formula / Definition
Effective Tax Rate | Income Taxes / Earnings Before Taxes
NOPAT | EBIT × (1 - Effective Tax Rate)
Operating NOWC | AR + Inventory + Prepaids - AP - Accrued Operating Liabilities
Change in NOWC | FY2025 Operating NOWC - FY2024 Operating NOWC
Textbook FCF | NOPAT + D&A - CapEx - Change in NOWC
Cash FCF | Cash Flow from Operations - CapEx
Other Operating Adjustments | Cash FCF - Textbook FCF
P&G Adjusted FCF | Cash FCF + 2017 U.S. Tax Act Payment Add-back
TABLE 4
Factor | Formula
Profit Margin | Net Earnings / Net Sales
Total Asset Turnover | Net Sales / Average Total Assets
Equity Multiplier | Average Total Assets / Average Total Shareholders' Equity
Direct ROE Check | Net Earnings / Average Total Shareholders' Equity
