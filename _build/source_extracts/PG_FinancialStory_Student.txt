Key Definitions |  |  |  | 
Operating NOWC | Accounts receivable + inventory + prepaids - accounts payable - accrued operating liabilities. | Excludes cash and interest-bearing debt.
Textbook FCF | NOPAT + D&A - capital expenditures - change in operating NOWC. | A simplified unlevered FCF measure used in FINC 310.
Cash FCF | Cash flow from operating activities - capital expenditures. | A direct cash-flow statement measure.
P&G Adjusted FCF | Operating cash flow - capital spending + 2017 U.S. Tax Act payments. | A company-defined non-GAAP measure; not identical to textbook FCF.
DuPont ROE | Profit margin × total asset turnover × equity multiplier. | Uses average FY2024/FY2025 assets and total shareholders' equity.
